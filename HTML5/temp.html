The Computer

A computer is an electronic device which is very important and useful for us. It has a CPU, a keyboard, a mouse, speakers, monitor and several wires. It runs on electricity. A computer has many uses. It is used for making documents, lists, software, invoices, etc. It is also used for doing calculations, playing games, accessing the internet, playing games, watching movies and listening to music. It can do almost anything. It has reduced the human efforts and hence made our life easy.

The Mobile

Mobile phones are also known as cell phones or mobile. Mobile phones increase communication networks. A mobile phone now performs the function of a computer called a smartphone. A smartphone with a camera can be used for video calling, photography, videography. One can connect with the world with a mobile phone with an internet connection. Mobile phone has become your personal computer where one can do all the activities just like a computer. You can watch movies on mobile phones, play games etc.
